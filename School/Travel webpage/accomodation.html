The Grace Hotel


Close to the Sydney Harbor Bridge and Sydney Opera House, The Grace Hotel offers luxury rooms and a rooftop fitness center with an indoor swimming pool and a sauna. The Grace is set in a historic building that has been lovingly restored in Sydney CBD (Central Business District). All rooms feature a flat-screen TV with cable channels and pay-per-view movies. They also offer free WiFi, tea/coffee maker, refrigerator and hairdryer. Some rooms include breakfast, which is served and enjoyed in your room. \ The Rocks and Circular Quay are 15 minutes' walk from The Grace Hotel. Shopping at the QVB and Pitt Street Mall is on the hotel’s doorstep.

The Sea World Resort

Located on Broadwater, with direct access to Sea World and only 15 minutes' walk from the popular Surfers Paradise and Marina Mirage Shopping area, this 4.5-star hotel features a 46 m lagoon pool and waterfall as well as an aquatic playground and 12-seater hot tub. Rooms at Sea World Resort are spacious and boast a contemporary décor. Each room is fitted with a balcony or patio which offers views of either the broadwater, pool or surrounding gardens. Guests enjoy free WiFi in all areas of the resort, as well as free wired internet in their room. At the resort's award-winning restaurant, Hatsuhana, guests can enjoy Japanese cuisine cooked Teppanyaki-style from fresh seafood and ingredients. The Lobby Lounge offers a variety of meals, from light snacks to delicious steaks. A la carte Breakfast & Dinner options are available across multiple restaurants.

Meriton Suites Broadbeach

Just 5 minutes’ walk from the beach, Meriton Suites Broadbeach offers luxurious accommodations. Leisure facilities include an indoor and outdoor pool, a sauna, hot tub and fitness center. Some suites feature stunning ocean views. Guests enjoy unlimited free WiFi at the property. Union Pay is accepted here. The air-conditioned suites have opening windows and laundry facilities. Each suite has an LCD TV with a DVD player on request. Studios have a kitchenette, and all other suites have a full kitchen. Meriton Suites Broadbeach is 5 minutes’ walk from The Star Casino and the Gold Coast Convention Center. Pacific Fair Shopping Center is a 10-minute walk away. You can relax in the sauna, or have a meal in the barbecue area. The outdoor deck is the perfect place to enjoy the Gold Coast sunshine. The apartment complex has a convenience store and restaurants. There are also a number of dining options within a 5-minute walk.
